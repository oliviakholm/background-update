Big Sky Hulu Drama Two sisters are kidnapped by a truck driver from a remote highway in Montana. Detectives find out they are not the only girls who have disappeared in the area and an unexpected person is involved.
Walking Dead Netflix Drama/Thriller/Horror Portrays life in the months and years following the zombie apocalypse. A group of survivors travel to find a safe and secure home. **This show is so much better than I expected, zombies arent my thing but I LOVED this show. Be prepared to cry a lot!
